About Us

Lesego Health Services provides technically innovative and reliable solutions to a diverse environment of Health Services. We believe that no project is too big or too small, throughout our practice as professional health givers, we refined the art of collaboration, further consultation and the art of engagement with the recipients of our services in the language that they understand. A network of highly professional and multi-lingual team stand ready to partner with Corporates , Government ,Non Governmental Organizations and individuals towards a healthy society.


Our company takes cognisance that we operate in a country where the quality health services is not a privilege to the overwhelming majority of the people, hence we strive to extend our services to a range of stakeholders that covers both the employed and the unemployed. We further believe that with information and education in the language and medium that is accessible to the clients, individuals can play a very important role in the overall health of the nation. We therefore adopt a developmental and educational approach to all our services.


We aim to get things right first the time and every time, and achieve 100% client satisfaction at every point in the process. Our people have broad range of skills in the health sector, project management and technical services. We proud ourselves of extensive and practical experience in Health Services, with a collective experience of more than 5 years in the fore front of giving care in Occupational Health and Primary Health Care space.
